Abdul Ghaffar Saeed

He was born on the 25th of April, 2001 at New Atuabo, a suburb within the Tarkwa-Nsuaem Municipality. His parents are Alhaji Saeed and Hajia Hawa.

Career

He started his footballing career with Barcelona Babies in 2006 as one of the clubs first ever players.

Due to his ball control prowess, his coach at the time, Spovis Haruna Yakubu, nicknamed him Kaka. His teammates often referred to him as Ronaldinho due to his exceptional dribbling ability.

He had his basic education at Golden Age School Complex, where he featured in school matches before continuing his studies in St. John's Boys Secondary School.

He had his Islamic education at Ummul Qura, where he was awarded a Certificate of Completion of the recitation of The Holy Quran.

He proceeded to study Arabic Language at Islamic Training Centre where he was elected Assistant School Prefect for the 2022/23 year group

He is a founding member of the Islamic Golden Eleven where he mostly feature as a central or attacking midfielder.

He enjoys playing FC25 Games at his leisure time.
