Yusuf Yakubu

He was born on the 26th of December, 2003 at New Atuabo, a suburb within the Tarkwa-Nsuaem Municipality. His parents are Abdulai and Salamatu Osman.

Career

He started his footballing career with Pages FC

He had his basic education at Delcy Montesorri before moving to New Atuabo Community Basic School (NATUCOBS), where he played for the school team in Inter-School Games. He furthered his studies at Kikam Technical Institute.

He currently studies at Kumasi Technical University (KsTU).

He is a founding member of the Islamic Golden Eleven where he specialises as a striker.

At his leisure time, he enjoys playing FC25 Games.
