Habil Alhassan

He was born on the 8th of April, 2002 at New Atuabo, a suburb within the Tarkwa-Nsuaem Municipality.

Career

He started his footballing career with Wa Power FC.

He had his basic education at Dan-Ibu International School.

He currently plays for Islamic Golden Eleven where he mostly feature as an attacker.

He enjoys playing football at his leisure time.
