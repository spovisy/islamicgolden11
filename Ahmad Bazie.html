Ahmad Bazie


He was born on the 25th of December, 2000 at Bondai, a suburb within the Prestea-Huni Valley Municipality. His parents are Alhaji Saeed and Hajia Hawa.

Career

He started his footballing career with Barcelona Babies in 2006 as one of the clubs first ever players.

He had his basic education at New Atuabo Community Basic School (NATUCOBS), where he featured in school

He is a founding member of the Islamic Golden Eleven where he mostly feature as a centre back or right back.

He enjoys playing FC25 Games at his leisure time.
